FAQ

Common questions.

Do I pay upfront?

We require 50% upfront and the remaining 50% on delivery. No full payment until you're happy.

What if I need changes?

Revisions are included in every plan. Additional changes beyond the included rounds are billed at a flat hourly rate.

Do you host the site?

We can help you set up hosting (recommended providers: Vercel, Netlify) or deploy directly to your existing host.

How do I pay?

We accept Mobile Money (MTN, Orange), float transfer, and Minipay. Payment details shared on inquiry.
